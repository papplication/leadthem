Lead Them

Lead Them is an action-adventure platformer game, setting place in a dark atmospheric microorganistic world. Your task is guide these friendly creatures to the source of food, and saving them from all the danger lurking for them. Designed for iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch, and requires iOS 8 or later.
Download for Apple iOS

‘Lead Them’ and they will follow

Lead Them is a dark atmospheric vertical-scrolling action adventure platformer, set in a wonderful microscopic world full of various inhabitants, organisms and little creatures. The player controls a group of the local dwellers to save and feed them, and discovers a particularly dangerous world on the way.

Lead Them takes vertical-scrollers to the next level with its innovative color and skill-based gameplay combined with stunning, atmospheric graphics and audio. This game is easy to learn and hard to master. Remember: Save Them! Feed Them! Lead Them!

  • 75 truly unique levels
  • Intuitive one-touch controls combined with innovative dynamic level design
  • Highly immersive audiovisual gaming experience
  • Various selectable skins with you can choose
  • iCloud support

Keep calm and Lead Them

Leading is easy as a pie, the player has to show the way to the tiny creatures in direction to food and survival using only his/her fingers, avoiding all the harmful barriers. What is more, multiple fingers can be used thus forming multiple swarms and by this boosting their chances to stay alive. By the escalation of the levels it automatically becames harder to carry forward and get the needed amount of food for them in the same time. Watch out! The game is easy to learn and hard to master!

Thanks for the randomly generated exciting and challenging universe the game is full of adventures. The player has a chance to discover diverse places leading along their tiny creatures in need for some protection, as they are not able to handle the difficulties rolling in the way.

Lead Them has relaxing background music and neat sound effects with vibration.
